ABOUT THE DEEDS LIMPEZA.

The Deeds Limpeza (Kenya) Ltd focused on delivering high quality and customized services to individuals, the private sector and governments in Kenya and from East Africa Countries. The company, was duly incorporated under the companies Act in the year 2017, began operations with a team of only two members of staff but has now grown to over two hundred and the initial two clients have multiplied many times over.
